Measurements & Ingredients

  • 6 hardboiled Eggs (boiled for 12 minutes and peeled)
  • 8 Fried Slices of Bacon (crispy)
  • 1 Pound (raw weight) of Chorizo (cooked)(reserve 2 tbs. of grease)
  • 2 Tbs. of Mayo
  • 1 Pinch of Salt/Pepper
  • 1 Tsp. of Red Pepper
  • 2 tsp. of Dried Parsley

Steps

  1. Blend eggs in food processor until coursely chopped
  2. Remove aggs from food processor
  3. Blend Bacon in food processor until coursely chopped
  4. Combine all ingredients in a bowl and mix by hand until completely incorporated

Enjoy!
